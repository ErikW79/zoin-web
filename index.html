Zoin is a cryptocurrency that has anonymity features based on the Zerocoin protocol.

http://spar.isi.jhu.edu/~mgreen/ZerocoinOakland.pdf



PoW Algo

- Lyra2Zoin - Lyra2(2, 330, 256)

- Memory intensive PoW uses an 8MB Lyra2 matrix



Block emission

- Initial reward: 100 ZOI

- Target time: 2.5 minutes

- Reward halves every 105000 blocks (six months)

- Tail emission of 10 ZOI after two years

- For first six months 20% of the block reward is allocated for Zoin development.

- Total coins: ~21 million plus tail emission after two years of ~10% per year

- Mined blocks mature in 120 blocks
